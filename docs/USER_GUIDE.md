# Harris Farm Hub — User Guide

> For the whole Harris Farm team. No technical knowledge required.

---

## What Is The Hub?

The Hub is Harris Farm Markets' AI Centre of Excellence — a single platform where you can access sales dashboards, customer analytics, property intelligence, AI tools, master data health, and more. Everything lives at one URL with one login.

**Live:** https://harris-farm-hub.onrender.com

---

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Open the Hub

Go to https://harris-farm-hub.onrender.com in your browser (desktop or mobile).

### Step 2: Enter the site password

You'll be asked for a site access code. This is a shared password given to you by your team lead. Enter it to proceed.

### Step 3: Create your account

Enter your email and create a personal password. This is your individual account — it tracks your progress, preferences, and role-based access.

### Step 4: Navigate

Use the top navigation bar to move between sections. Your role determines which pages you can see.

## Key Pages Explained

### Sales

Weekly performance dashboard. See KPIs (revenue, profit, margin, transactions, avg basket), store comparisons, and revenue trends. Use filters to drill into specific stores, time periods, or product categories.

### Profitability

P&L waterfall analysis. See the breakdown from revenue through to net profit — COGS, labour, rent, utilities. Compare stores side-by-side and spot margin opportunities.

### Customer Hub

Customer intelligence and segmentation. Understand who your customers are, how often they shop, what they buy, and how segments are trending over time.

### Store Network

Store profiles with trade area demographics, performance metrics, and location intelligence. Part of the Property section — view all 31+ stores with detailed profiles.

### Skills Academy (Growing Legends)

AI learning platform with XP system, daily challenges, and leaderboards. Build your AI skills through structured modules and earn badges as you progress.

### Prompt Engine

Create AI-powered outputs, score them against the rubric, and submit for approval. Design custom analytical queries without writing code.

### MDHE Dashboard

Master Data Health Engine — view data quality scores across domains (product, supplier, pricing, store), validation results, and track issues through resolution.

### Hub Assistant

Ask questions about Harris Farm in plain English. Powered by 545 knowledge articles covering strategy, operations, and more. Get instant answers without searching through documents.

## Role-Based Access

Your account has a role that determines which pages you can see:

- **Admin** — Full access to all 43 pages, including User Management and Back of House
- **Standard** — Access to Strategy, Growing Legends, and Operations pages
- **MDHE** — Additional access to MDHE pages for data quality management

If you can't see a page you need, contact your admin to update your role.

Users listed in the SLT email list are automatically promoted to admin on login.

## Your Monday Morning Workflow

1. Open the Hub at https://harris-farm-hub.onrender.com
2. Go to **Sales** — check weekend performance
3. Look at the KPI row: revenue, profit, margin, transactions, avg basket
4. Scan the revenue trend chart — any drops?
5. Check store comparisons — who's up, who's down?
6. If a store looks off, switch to **Profitability** for the P&L drill-down
7. Use the **Hub Assistant** to ask specific questions in plain English

---

## Tips and Best Practices

1. **Start with the KPIs** — they tell you if something needs attention
2. **Use filters to narrow down** — don't try to look at everything at once
3. **Ask the Hub Assistant** — it's faster than building a custom report
4. **Check Skills Academy** — daily challenges keep your AI skills sharp
5. **Use the comparison toggle** — "vs Last Year" and "vs Budget" reveal trends
6. **Save useful prompts** in the Prompt Engine so your team can reuse them

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Can't see a page | Your role may not have access. Contact your admin to check your permissions. |
| Page shows an error | Try refreshing the page (Ctrl+R / Cmd+R). If the error persists, the backend may still be starting up — wait 30 seconds and refresh again. |
| Can't log in | Make sure you have the correct site password. If you've forgotten your account password, use the password reset feature or contact your admin. |
| Data looks empty | Data files may not be loaded yet. If you just opened the Hub after a deploy, wait a minute for the backend to finish starting. |
| Hub is slow to load | The first load after a period of inactivity may take 30-60 seconds (cold start). Subsequent loads are fast. |
| Need help | Contact hub-support@harrisfarm.com or reach out on Slack. |
