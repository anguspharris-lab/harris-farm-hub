# Branding Rules — Harris Farm Hub

## Logo
- Logo is at `assets/logo.png` (already copied)
- Add to ALL dashboards top-left: `st.image("assets/logo.png", width=200)`
- Consistent across all 19 dashboards

## Brand Colours
- HFM Green: `#4ba021` (primary brand, Pillar 5 accent)
- HFM Dark: `#171819` (text/headings)
- HFM Background: `#f5f5f5` (page background — light theme)
- Card background: white with `box-shadow: 0 1px 4px rgba(0,0,0,0.06)`
- Defined in `dashboards/shared/styles.py`

## Goal Colours (goals_config.py)
- G1 Strategy: `#2d8659`
- G2 Data: `#3b82f6`
- G3 Superstars: `#a855f7`
- G4 Supply Chain: `#f59e0b`
- G5 Improve: `#ef4444`
- Badge opacity: append `20` to hex for background, `50` for border

## Pillar Colours (nav.py)
- P1 Greater Goodness: `#16a34a`
- P2 Customer: `#7c3aed`
- P3 People: `#059669`
- P4 Operations: `#d97706`
- P5 Digital & AI: `#4ba021`

## Design Principles
- Light theme throughout (NOT dark navy) — all 19 dashboards use `HFM_BG=#f5f5f5`
- Green gradient hero banners (`border-radius:14px`)
- Colored left-border accent on cards
- "For The Greater Goodness" tagline in mission statement
- Warm, friendly tone — "Harris Farmers" not "employees"
